Tax From US

Step by step we are developing and realizing the digital strategy
for US based financial startup. Take a closer look at our approach.

We have started our work with logo. We wanted it to be bright and powerful, and, of course, associated with the US.

Target audience, clients of this startup, are individuals who come to the US for a studying or seasonal job and have the right to receive the tax refund when they return to their home countries. This audience is young and open-minded. So we used elements of the American flag and implemented the name of the startup, to create some fresh label like logotype that will be easily transformed into badge or sticker. Moreover, we worked a lot with the fonts.

Website creation

This website is just the digital iceberg. Part of the website you see at the top, not being logged in, is also huge, but that is only 10% of the service.

So what is the service - it is the sophisticated digital algorithm that collects personal data of the user and calculates the specified amount of money wich should be refunded to a customer.

Programmed on PHP using Phalcon framework network we've reached solid speed and scalability edge. We've implemented useful partnership tools that enable the new generation of b2b business processes for the company.

Among outstanding features of the website are HTML to pdf automatic conversion (b2c), the module for agencies' base dumping and analysis tools (b2b), payment processing integrations and IRS processes integration.

We worked on this project for eight months. And now it is in the stage of active support and development. In January 2017 we've started the marketing support for companies online strategy. We are encouraged to get first results and perform our job in the high-level pace.

For the moment our responsibilities are the marketing strategy, email marketing, digital marketing, creative design and viral marketing.
