CFC Consulting Company

CFC Consulting - is the international company based in Dubai and Kyiv. CFC operates in the spheres of public and government relations, media relations, investor relations and event-management.

Brand Identity (digital identity)

CFC is in the market for more than ten years, but their brand identity was a little bit old for the moment and needed some renovations. Initially, we were invited to work only with logo. But while reinventing CFC’s new brand logo, we arrived at the decision to create entirely new brand digital identity and website.

From the very beginning we understood that we need to apply the logo to real visit cards and marketing materials, so the company proposed us to play with one of the cornerstones of the CFC’s philosophy - meridians (CFC means Club of Five Continents).

So we used Meridian pattern in design all corporate identity:

Website design and development

Meridians became the cornerstone for website designing also. We used pulsating meridians at the preloader and the first screen of the site.

The whole web site is light and white. We tried to leave the maximum of free space to let the website breathe, despite the fact that PR consulting company wants and has what to say to potential customers.

We used lots of animated design solutions to avoid boring materials like shatter stock photos etc.

We were pleased to have CFC as the customers, because we know that they have among their customers such brands like UBER, Samsung, Telenor, SAP, Google, etc.

For the moment CFC is our strategic partners in the regions of Europe and the Middle East.

We are providing technical support to the company.
We are involved in CFC’s digital strategy with our design expertise.
